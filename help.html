What Do We Do?

Many people find themselves in a situation where they're unsure of what to do or where to go. Do any of the following sound familiar?

  • My son, a black male, just snapped. Who do I call?
  • I am scared to call the police. If I call the police, what do I say so they don’t come in unaware of our situation? Is there anybody else to call besides the police?
  • My child is an adult, and I know they are not in their right state of mind, but they won’t sign a release form. How do I get information?
  • My adult child doesn’t have insurance or they have insurance that doesn’t cover the type of care they needs. What resources are there to help me with out of pocket costs?
  • My adult child has run away. How can I get around the 48 hour missing person rule?
  • I don’t believe my loved one is sick.
  • My loved one has Alzheimer’s. My family does not believe in putting a family member in a home, but I have primary responsibility. What should I do?
  • "Going to a therapist is for ‘weak’ people who can’t handle things on their own." So how do I make my child stronger?
  • How can I go about gaining legal guardianship for my adult child?
